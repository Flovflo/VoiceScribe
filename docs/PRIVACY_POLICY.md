# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

## Overview

VoiceScribe is a macOS dictation app that records audio only when you explicitly start recording. Transcription runs locally on-device using MLX models.

## Data we process

- Microphone audio while you are actively recording
- Your selected model, microphone, and language preferences
- Optional local model files cached on your Mac for offline transcription

## What we do not collect

- We do not require an account.
- We do not run analytics or ad tracking.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not send transcription audio to a remote speech-to-text service.

## Model downloads

On first use, VoiceScribe may download the selected speech model from Hugging Face over HTTPS and store it locally in your app cache.

## Clipboard behavior

- Standard distribution: VoiceScribe copies the transcript to the clipboard and can automatically trigger paste into the active app.
- App Store distribution: VoiceScribe copies the transcript to the clipboard only. You paste it manually.

## Permissions

VoiceScribe requests microphone permission so it can record audio for transcription.

## Data retention

- Recorded audio is processed for the active transcription flow and is not uploaded for cloud transcription.
- Cached model files remain on your Mac until you remove the app cache or uninstall the app.
