## Promotional Text

Fast local dictation for macOS with on-device MLX speech recognition.

## Description

VoiceScribe is a fast macOS dictation app built for local speech-to-text.

- Record with a keyboard shortcut or from the menu bar
- Keep transcription on-device with local MLX models
- Choose the language manually for better recognition accuracy
- Pick the model size that fits your Mac
- Copy the transcript straight to the clipboard

VoiceScribe is designed for quick capture without a bulky editor window. Open the HUD, speak, and keep typing in your destination app.
